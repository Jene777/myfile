    Admission Requirements


    For Freshman

  • Form 138 (High School Rating Card)

  • Alternative Learning Center (ALS) Rating for ALS Graduate.

  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (from the Guidance Counselor of the school last attended)

  • Copy of Birth Certificate from National Statistics Office (NSO)

  • Three (3) copies 2" x 2" and 1 copy 1" x 1" colored pictures with white background

For Transferee

  • Certified True Copy of Grades (from the institution last attended).

  • Certificate of Honorable Dismissal.

  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (from the Guidance Counselor of the school last attended).

For Foreign Students

  • Certificate Of Completion of Secondary Curriculum.

  • Original Transcript of Records

  • Personal Data

  • Affidavit of Support

  • Alien Certificate of Registration(ACR)

  • Result of TOEFL/IELTS(for non-native speaker of English)

  • Student Visa

  • Security Clearance

  • Resident Guarantor

  • Ched Permit

  • Alien Fee
